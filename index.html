About Pinnacle Construction

Pinnacle Construction and Projects is a dynamic construction firm based in Kempton Park, serving clients throughout South Africa. Established in 2023, the company leverages over a decade of experience in the industry to offer tailored turnkey solutions in asphalt surfacing, road construction, and brick paving.

Renowned for professionalism, safety, and high-quality workmanship, Pinnacle is committed to delivering innovative solutions with premium materials. Our diverse range of services caters to residential, commercial, and municipal clients, ensuring satisfaction and excellence in every project.

Our expert asphalt (tar) and brick paving solutions cater to all your paving needs. With our locally approved services for quality and peace of mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you service?

Pinnacle Construction and Projects is based in Kempton Park, and we proudly serve clients throughout the entire Gauteng province and across South Africa for larger projects. Contact us to confirm if we service your specific location.

How long does a typical paving project take?

The duration of a project depends on its size and complexity. A standard residential driveway can often be completed within 2-4 days, while larger commercial parking lots or road sections will take longer. We provide a detailed project timeline with every quote.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, absolutely. We stand by the quality of our workmanship and materials. All our projects come with a comprehensive warranty. The specific terms and duration of the warranty will be clearly outlined in your project contract.

What materials do you use?

We use only high-quality, SABS-approved materials from reputable suppliers to ensure the longevity and durability of our projects, whether it's asphalt, paving bricks, or building supplies.
